About Me

Design Shaped
By Precision

I'm Victor Ugborgu — a freelance web designer based in Nigeria, and an optometrist who made a deliberate shift into digital design. What I carry from healthcare into every project is an obsession with detail, clarity, and purpose.

In optometry, a fraction of a millimetre matters. In design, a pixel does too. That mindset shapes how I approach every project — from the layout of a school homepage to the copy on a business page. Nothing is accidental.

My work focuses on schools, businesses, medical professionals, startups, and brands who want a strong, credible online presence without the corporate price tag. I build websites that are fast, beautiful, and built to convert.

Questions

Common
Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Message me directly on WhatsApp — I respond quickly.

💬 Ask on WhatsApp
How long does a website project take? +
Most projects take between 1 to 4 weeks depending on scope. A simple 5-page business website may be ready in 7–10 days. Larger, more complex projects with custom features take 3–4 weeks. I'll give you a realistic timeline at the start.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Absolutely. Website redesign is one of my most common services. I'll review your current site, understand what's not working, and rebuild it from the ground up with a modern, effective design.
Will my website work on mobile phones? +
Yes — every website I build is mobile-first and fully responsive. It will look and work great on all screen sizes: phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. This is non-negotiable in my process.
Do you help with hosting and domain setup? +
Yes. I provide guidance on choosing the right hosting and domain registrar for your budget and needs. I can also handle the full setup if needed, so your website is live and configured correctly from day one.
Can I update the website content myself? +
Depending on the platform used, yes. If you need a CMS (Content Management System) for easy self-editing, I can build with that in mind. I'll also provide a brief handover guide so you're comfortable managing your site.
Do you work with schools and churches? +
Yes — schools, churches, NGOs, and community organizations are welcome clients. I understand the unique communication needs of these institutions and design accordingly, with clarity and trust at the center.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance? +
Yes. After launch, I offer monthly maintenance plans for content updates, security patches, backups, and technical support. This keeps your website performing reliably over time without you worrying about technical issues.
